Questions & Answers

Everything you might be wondering.

Straightforward answers about how we work, what it costs, and what to expect.

What exactly does Go Systematic do?

We help businesses run smoother by finding the right automation and AI tools, setting them up properly, and training your team to use them confidently. We are not just resellers, we work hands-on with you from discovery to implementation to ongoing support, so the tools actually deliver results instead of gathering dust.

Do I need to be technical to work with you?

Not at all. Most of our clients are business owners, not tech people. That is exactly why we exist. We handle the technical setup and configuration, then coach you and your team in plain language until everything feels natural. You focus on your business, we handle the systems.

What size businesses do you work with?

Anyone with a team of 5 or more, plus freelancers and solo entrepreneurs who want to scale. Whether you are an MSME, a startup, or a growing business, our tools and approach adapt to your size and needs.

Can I try the tools before committing?

Absolutely. Every platform we recommend, you can try free first, no credit card needed. Check out our Try Free section to sign up for the Automate Team App, Sales CRM, Forms, or Chats. Explore them yourself, and we are happy to help you get set up.

What is your relationship with Automate Business?

We work in close collaboration with Automate Business, founded by Mr. Kewal Kishan. Their platform is a core part of what we offer, and we know it inside out. Think of us as your on-the-ground implementation and support partner, we live and breathe these tools every day so you do not have to figure them out alone.

What are AI Calling Agents and AI Avatars?

AI Calling Agents are voice-powered assistants that handle inbound and outbound phone calls, qualifying leads, answering questions, and booking appointments 24/7. AI Avatars are realistic AI video presenters you can use for training, marketing, or customer communication without needing a camera or studio. We help you set both up for your specific use case.

How much does it cost to work with you?

It depends on what you need, the tools involved, the scope of setup, and the level of ongoing support. We always start with a free discovery call to understand your situation, then give you a clear, honest proposal. No hidden fees, no pressure. Reach out and we will figure out what makes sense for you.

Do you help with WhatsApp Business API setup?

Yes, this is one of our specialities. We handle the full WhatsApp Business API onboarding, from Meta business verification to message template creation and automation flows. We make sure you are compliant and ready to message customers professionally at scale.

How long does it take to get started?

Many businesses have their first automation live within a day of getting started. A full rollout across multiple modules and team training typically takes a couple of weeks, depending on complexity. We move at a pace that works for you, never rushing you into things you are not ready for.

What if my team resists using new tools?

This is more common than you would think, and it is exactly why hands-on training matters. We do not just hand over logins. We coach your team directly, show them how the tools make their work easier, and stay available for questions until adoption feels natural. When people see the tools save them time, resistance fades fast.
